Disconnecting This App

This App has no hosted account system since it is a personal-use, single-user tool. To disconnect it from your QuickBooks Online company:

  1. Sign in to your Intuit account at developer.intuit.com or your QuickBooks Online company settings.
  2. Navigate to connected apps and revoke access for this application, or delete the stored OAuth refresh token locally on the device running the App.

See the Privacy Policy for more information on data handling.
