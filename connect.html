Connecting / Reconnecting This App

This App has no hosted account system since it is a personal-use, single-user tool. To (re)authorize it with your QuickBooks Online company:

  1. Run the App's local authorization script on the device it's installed on.
  2. You will be redirected to QuickBooks Online to log in and grant access.
  3. Once authorized, the App stores the resulting tokens locally to make future API calls.

See the Privacy Policy for more information on data handling.
