Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This application ("the App") is a personal-use tool with a single user: its developer. This policy describes how the App handles data.

What data the App accesses

  • QuickBooks Online transaction data, limited to bank/credit card transactions marked as "excluded" within a specified date range, accessed via the QuickBooks Online Accounting API.
  • Access is read-only. The App does not create, edit, or delete any QuickBooks Online data.

Where the data goes

  • Summarized monthly totals are written to a private Google Sheets spreadsheet owned by the developer.
  • No data is shared with, sold to, or accessible by any third party.
  • No data is used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than the developer's personal expense tracking.

Data storage and security

  • OAuth credentials (access and refresh tokens) are stored locally on the developer's own device and are not transmitted to any third-party server.
  • Error logs may contain transaction metadata and are stored locally for troubleshooting purposes only.

Data retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to perform the monthly summary and is not archived beyond what's needed for troubleshooting.

Contact

As this is a personal-use application with a single user, questions about this policy can be directed to the developer directly.
